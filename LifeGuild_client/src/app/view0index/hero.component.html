Gamify Your Life!

LifeGuild is a free productivity app that helps you keep you motivated and on track for tasks using game mechanics.

Adopt Cute Pets!

Whenever you finish a task, you can earn cute pets that heal you in game life, just like real life pets do.

Boss Project Fights!

Have a big project? That's a boss level monster! Break up your tasks into managable subtasks and fight with your team!

Earn rewards by doing tasks!

Relax and chill with your favourite Netflix show with the assurance that you've accomplished everything for the day.
